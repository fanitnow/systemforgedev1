# Outreach Strategy

## Week 1 Target: First 5 Clients

### Method 1 — Instagram DM (Cold)

Send this to local business owners:

> Quick question — do you have anything set up to automatically follow up with people who reach out but don't book?
>
> I help businesses like yours find and fix the spots where they're losing customers. Takes about 7 minutes and I can show you exactly where the gaps are.
>
> Would it be okay if I sent you a quick audit? No pitch, just the info.

### Method 2 — Walk-In (Fastest Money)

Target: barbershops, salons, auto shops, gyms during slow periods.

Script:
> "Hey — I work with businesses on their backend systems. Quick question: when someone calls or DMs you and doesn't book, what happens after that?"

Let them answer. Then:

> "That's the most common place businesses lose money — no system to follow up automatically. I built a tool that audits exactly where that's happening. Takes 7 minutes. Want me to run it right now?"

### Method 3 — Free Audit Upsell

1. Offer free audit at systemforgeai.com
2. Show them their report
3. Say: "I can fix all of this for you this week for $750."

## Objection Handling Quick Reference

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|---------|
| Too expensive | "You're losing more than this monthly. The fix pays for itself in week one." |
| Need to think | "What specifically? I can answer right now." |
| I already have tools | "Tools aren't the problem — they're not wired together." |
| Wrong time | "Your systems leak money every day this isn't fixed." |
| Do it myself | "You could. But it'll take months. We do it in 48 hours." |
