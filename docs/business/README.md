# Business Model

## Service Tiers

| Tier | Product | Price | Delivery | Margin |
|------|---------|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Audit Report | $97 | 24 hours | ~90% |
| 2 | System Starter | $750 | 48–72 hours | ~85% |
| 3 | Full System Build | $2,500 | 5–7 days | ~75% |

## Revenue Targets

| Timeframe | Target | What Gets You There |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| Week 1 | $1,500–$3,500 | 2–5 System Starter clients |
| Month 1 | $5,000–$10,000 | Mix of Tier 2 + 1–2 Full Builds |
| Month 3 | $15,000–$25,000 | Referrals + repeat business |
| Month 6 | $30,000+ | VA handling builds, you close |
| Year 1 | $100,000–$250,000 | Scaled outreach + team |

## Target Verticals (Priority Order)

1. Barbershops / Salons — fastest close, clear pain, $750 sweet spot
2. Personal Trainers — easiest pitch, daily problem
3. Contractors / Trades — highest ticket, biggest gaps
4. Real Estate Agents — ROI obvious, follow-up is their business
5. Wellness / Med Spas — high ticket, repeat business
6. Coaches / Consultants — understand ROI language, full build buyers

## Delivery Infrastructure

For Tier 2 builds, the delivery stack is:
- **GoHighLevel** — CRM, lead capture, follow-up sequences, booking
- **Zapier** (alternative) — same capabilities, higher cost
- **Loom** — walkthrough video delivery
- **Calendly** (or GHL booking) — handoff call scheduling

When volume exceeds 3 builds/week, hire a VA at $15–20/hr on Upwork to execute builds using documented templates.
